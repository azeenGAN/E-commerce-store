Welcome to Adore Candles

At Adore Candles, we take pride in creating exquisite, handcrafted candles that add a touch of warmth and elegance to your space. Each candle is a labor of love, meticulously crafted from scratch, ensuring a unique and authentic experience for our customers.

Our journey contains struggles and a bit of failures. But not letting them stopping me.

Our Craftsmanship

Our candles are made with a passion for creativity and a commitment to quality. We source the finest ingredients, using a premium blend of Coconut-Apricot wax and natural cotton wicks. This exclusive blend not only provides a clean and even burn but also enhances the fragrance experience, creating a soothing atmosphere in any room.

Unveiling Nature's Goodness

Adore Candles are crafted with sustainability in mind. Our wax is not only gluten-free but also free from toxins, parabens, and phthalates. We prioritize using renewable and sustainable materials to contribute to a healthier environment.

Customization is Key

We believe in the power of personalization. Adore Candles offers a range of scents and colors that you can mix and match to create a candle that reflects your unique style and preferences. Each candle is a canvas, waiting for you to unleash your creativity.

Reduce, Reuse, Recreate

Candle-making is not just a craft; it's a commitment to reducing waste. Adore Candles encourages the reuse of materials, allowing you to repurpose old containers or choose materials that are easily recyclable. Join us in our mission to contribute to a more sustainable future.
